What is ZOFFY's return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Please ensure that the product is in its original condition and packaging. For more details, visit our Returns & Refunds page.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by clicking on the tracking link sent to your email after your purchase. Alternatively, you can visit our Order Tracking page and enter your order number and email address.

Does ZOFFY ship internationally?

Yes, ZOFFY ships to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. For more information, visit our Shipping Information page.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. For more details on payment methods, please visit our Payment Options page.

How can I contact ZOFFY customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by phone at +91 99408 12526 or by email at support@zoffy.com. We're available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM .
